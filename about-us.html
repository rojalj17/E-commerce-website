About Us

From Heart

It began as a dream four years ago, fueled by my passion for fashion and a love for styling that started six years ago as a social media influencer. My followers always appreciated my unique dressing sense, and after receiving great feedback from my collaborations with various brands, I decided to create my own. I'll never forget the day I decided to start my own brand. I was afraid, wondering if I had enough to make it happen. But my mom, with her unwavering belief in me, said, "We don't know where we can go without trying." Those words have stayed with me and continue pushing me forward daily. And that's how Wardrobe by Meenu was born—a brand built on passion and a belief in chasing dreams.

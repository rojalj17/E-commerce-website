Refund Policy

Here, we offer high-quality products and ensure a positive shopping experience for all our customers. However, we understand that sometimes returns or exchanges are necessary. Please review our return and refund policy below:

Returns: We regret to inform you that we do not offer a return option for any of our products. We encourage you to carefully review your order before making a purchase.
Replacements: We provide replacement options for products that arrive damaged. If you receive a damaged item, please follow these steps:
Report the Issue: Contact our customer service team within 48 hours of receiving the damaged product. Provide your order number, a brief description of the damage, and photos of the damaged item.
Assessment: Our team will review your report and the provided evidence to assess the damage.
Replacement Approval: Once your claim is approved, we will arrange for a replacement of the damaged product. Please note that replacement requests are subject to product availability.
Exclusions: Damage caused by improper handling or misuse by the customer.
Products that have been used or altered.
We appreciate your understanding and cooperation. For further assistance, feel free to contact our customer service team.

1. Return Eligibility
- Items must be returned within eight days of the delivery date.
- To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, unworn, and in the same condition that you received it. The item should also be in its original packaging with tags attached.
- Items that are damaged, washed, or altered in any way will not be eligible for return or exchange.

2. Non-Returnable Items
The following items are not eligible for return or exchange:
- Custom or personalized.
- Sale or clearance items.
- Gift cards.

3. Return Process
To initiate a return, please follow these steps:
1. Contact Us:
Email our customer support at meenuvmacademy@gmail.com with your order number and reason for the return.
2. Approval:
Once your return is approved, we will provide you with return instructions and a shipping address.
3. Ship the Item:
You are responsible for the shipping costs of returning your item. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

4. Refunds
Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will send you an email notification regarding the status of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

5. Exchanges
If you need a different size or color, you can request an exchange. Follow the same process as a return, and once we receive your item, we will ship the replacement.

6. Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive an item that is damaged or defective, please get in touch with us within 48 hours of delivery. We will cover the return shipping costs for damaged or faulty items and provide a replacement or a full refund.

7. Late or Missing Refunds
If you haven't received a refund within the stated time frame:
- First, recheck your bank account.
- Contact your credit card company or bank, as processing may take some time.
- If you’ve done all this and still have not received your refund, please contact us at brand@gmail.com.

8. Shipping Costs
- Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility, except for cases where the product is defective or incorrect.
- Shipping costs are non-refundable.

Privacy policy

Here, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and make a purchase. Please read this policy carefully to understand how your personal information is handled.

1. Information We Collect
We collect personal and non-personal information from you when you interact with our website or make a purchase. This includes:

Personal Information:
- Contact Details: Your name, email address, billing address, shipping address, and phone number.
- Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, payment method, and transaction data (handled securely by our payment processors).
- Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your username, password, and purchase history.

Non-Personal Information:
- Browser and Device Information: Your IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring website.
- Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including the pages you view and the products you search for.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
- Process Transactions: Handle your orders, process payments, and deliver products to you.
- Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries, provide assistance, and handle returns or refunds.
- Marketing and Promotions: Send you promotional offers, updates on new products, and marketing communications (only if you have opted in).
- Website Improvement: Analyze how our website is used, improve its functionality, and enhance user experience.
- Security and Fraud Prevention: Protect against unauthorized access, fraud, and other illegal activities.

3. How We Share Your Information
We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners to facilitate our services, including:

- Service Providers: Payment processors, shipping partners, email marketing services, and customer support platforms.
- Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our business, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
- Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to legal processes, such as a subpoena or court order.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. These cookies help us:

- Remember your preferences and shopping cart contents.
- Track website usage and analyze traffic patterns.
- Display personalized ads based on your browsing history.
You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.

5. Data Security
We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement industry-standard security measures to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. This includes encrypting (SSL) for payment processing and securing our website infrastructure.
However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is entirely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its security.

6. Your Rights
You have the following rights regarding your personal information:
- Access: You may request access to the personal information we hold about you.
- Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
- Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal obligations.
- Opt-Out of Marketing: You may opt out of receiving marketing emails by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any of our emails.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at brand@gmail.com.

7. Data Retention
We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. If you request the deletion of your data, we will comply unless we are legally required to retain it.

8. Third-Party Links
Our website may contain links to third-party websites. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those websites, and we are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

9. Children's Privacy
Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected such information, please get in touch with us, and we will take steps to delete it.

10. International Users
If you are accessing our website from outside India, please note that your information may be transferred, stored, and processed in the country where our servers are located. By using our website, you consent to transferring your information to our servers in India.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes. Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
